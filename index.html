Features of the Case App

User Friendly Interface

CASE enables community members to create accounts effortlessly and upload details like videos and pictures about their cases in their respective communites. A user has the option to use AI to improve their post during upload which helps in better communication and reduces grammatical errors

Community

By using GPS to determine a user's location, we can identify their primary community, which is typically their state. Within each state, there are smaller communities comprising various towns and places. When a user wants to upload their content, they can do so within one of the communities of their state. They also have the option to create a new community if it doesn't already exist. All communities and sub-communities are organized based on geographical location.‚Äù
